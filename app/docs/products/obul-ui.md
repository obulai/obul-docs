:::tip Obul Login — Coming Soon

**The easiest way to add payments to your frontend.**

Obul Login is a drop-in widget that wraps your API in a beautiful, embeddable payment UI. Your users authenticate, pay with cards, and you get paid automatically via x402 — while you maintain full control over your frontend experience.

**What you'll get:**
- Ready-made payment widget (just embed it)
- Built-in user management and authentication
- Cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay support
- You receive payments via x402 (no intermediaries)
- Works with any backend — just point Obul at your API

Perfect for developers who want to monetize APIs without building billing infrastructure from scratch.

**Coming Q3 2025**
:::
