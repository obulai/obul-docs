**The easiest way to add payments to your frontend.**

Obul Login is a drop-in widget that provides embeddable login and payment allowing users access to access your x402 end-point from a frontend. Your users authenticate, pay with cards, and you get paid automatically via x402 — while you maintain full control over your frontend experience.

**What you'll get:**
- Ready-made login and payment widget (just embed it)
- Built-in user management and authentication
- You receive payments via x402 (no intermediaries)

Perfect for developers who want own their user experince without building billing infrastructure from scratch.

:::
